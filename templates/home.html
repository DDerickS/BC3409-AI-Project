About Us

This Webpage is using Machine Learning Model to predict whether an SMS or email is real or fake. Members of the public must be vigilant and must exercise caution when making any online transactions, and exercise due diligence to protect themselves from falling victim to scams. Individuals should not divulge their personal information, especially their financial information, to anyone, even those posing as representatives of the police or other law enforcement agencies.

If you need any advice, please call the National Crime Prevention Council Anti-Scam Helpline at 1800-722-6688 or go to www.scamalert.sg. To fight against scame, you may wish to sign up Scam Alert's Telegram or WhatsApp over at www.scamalert.sg/fight.
